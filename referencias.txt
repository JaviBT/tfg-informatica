Pavement Distress Identification Based on Computer Vision
and Controller Area Network (CAN) Sensor Models:

The computer vision model was developed using the You Only
Look Once (YOLOv5) algorithm for detecting and classifying pavement distresses into nine classes.
The sensor-based model combined eight Controller Area Network (CAN) bus sensors available
in most new vehicles to predict pavement distress. This research employed an extreme gradient
boosting model (XGBoost) to train the sensor-based model.
